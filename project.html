Mera Kisan Mitra

An AI-powered platform that helps farmers detect wheat leaf diseases from a single photo of the affected plant — identifying the disease and recommending treatment, suitable pesticides, dosage, and estimated cost.

By catching disease early, it cuts crop losses, improves yield, and reduces unnecessary pesticide use — a faster and cheaper alternative to manual diagnosis.

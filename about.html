01 — Where it starts Class 1–4

Early Schooling

Amit began his educational journey at a local private school in Ballarpur, where he completed Classes 1 and 2. Due to financial constraints — and to give both children in the family equal educational opportunities — his parents later enrolled him in a government school. That decision became an important part of his journey: it taught him, early, the value of money and how much it shapes the quality of an education.

02 — The years that shaped him Class 5–10

Primary & Secondary Schooling

Amit completed Class 5 through Class 10 at Nagar Parishad Gandhi Vidyalaya, Ballarpur. These years played a significant role in shaping his personality and values. He was fortunate to receive constant support, guidance, and encouragement from teachers who cared for him like family. He remains deeply grateful to every one of them — for the part they played in his growth, academic and personal.

03 — A dream, and a deadline 2020 – 2022

Higher Secondary Education

Amit completed Classes 11 and 12 at Nehru Vidyalaya & Junior Science College. Across these two years he dedicated himself to preparing for the NEET examination, with the dream of one day pursuing an MBBS degree. He worked sincerely — but was unable to secure a government MBBS seat. Around four to five months before the examination, unexpected circumstances affected both his preparation and his life — something he chooses to keep private.

The Turning Point

The lowest, longest year.

This phase became one of the most challenging periods of Amit's life. He experienced severe depression, uncontrollable thoughts, and constant mental migraines. It took nearly a year to recover and regain emotional stability. Despite being one of the lowest points in his life, this experience transformed him — into someone stronger, more resilient, and far more grateful for what came after.

— recovery took its time, and that was allowed to be true —

04 — Rebuilding, in public 2022 – 2026

Undergraduate Studies

Amit completed his B.Tech in Computer Technology from Priyadarshini College of Engineering, Nagpur. These four years were a period of exploration and self-discovery. He made plenty of mistakes, learned from each one, and chased every opportunity he could reach with the knowledge and experience he had. They taught him the value of a good education, a good environment, and a constant learning attitude — lessons that have stayed with him since.

05 — Where the thread is now 2026 — Present

Postgraduate, at IIT Kanpur

Amit is currently pursuing his M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kanpur. He doesn't think of this as an achievement so much as a privilege and a responsibility. He sees it as a door opened onto continuous learning, research, and personal growth — one that motivates him to learn something new every day.

Chapter One

Pradnya Khobragade

Government School Teacher, Nagar Parishad Gandhi Vidyalaya, Ballarpur

Pradnya Ma'am is one of the most important people in my life. She played a major role in shaping my character from Class 5 to Class 10, caring for me with the love and affection of a mother. While I was in Class 10, I used to study at her home until late at night. She not only taught me Science but also gave me emotional support and the confidence to believe in myself.

She inspired me to dream big and motivated me to prepare for the GATE examination. Whatever I have achieved today and whatever I achieve in the future is because of her blessings, guidance and support. I will always be grateful to her.

To the teacher who cared for me like a mother and shaped the person who I am today.

Chapter Two

Rishav Bhaiyya

Gazetted Officer at a High Court

I connected with Rishav Bhaiya in September 2025 while I was struggling with my GATE preparation. He supported and guided me throughout the final phase of my GATE journey and even after the examination.Bhaiya constantly messaged me, telling me what to do and what not to do, which subjects to complete, when to start each subject, which question sets to practice, how to analyze tests and the importance of PYQs. More than that Bhaiya was more serious about my preparation than I was which motivated me to push my limits.

His mentorship played a significant role in helping me achieve my goal of securing admission to IIT Kanpur for M.Tech in CSE. His impact on my life and career will always be remembered, and I will always be grateful to him.

A mentor plants seeds of courage, discipline, and hope without expecting anything in return. Years later, those seeds grow into meaningful lives and fulfilled dreams.
